WHO WE ARE

Francisco Munoz-Corral has been making furniture (originally with his father in Mexico) for over 50 years.

He created Corral Rustic Furniture to share his love of hard and softwoods and create an extensive collection of furnishings and home goods that are constantly evolving to fit our customers’ needs.
